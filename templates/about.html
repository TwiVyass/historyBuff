About

Welcome to HauteBot — your conversational co-creator for fashion ideas, references, and design inspiration.
This project was born from a simple need: to help fashion students and design enthusiasts get quick, reliable answers — especially when they don’t have easy access to mentors, libraries, or endless time to scroll through search results.

Whether you’re stuck on a historical detail, a silhouette, or just need a spark of creativity, HauteBot is here to help you move forward.
It’s not just about automation — it’s about assistance. Think of HauteBot as a chat companion trained in the language of fashion, built to help you brainstorm, explore, and experiment faster.

While some AI tools generate images, we’ve chosen not to — out of respect for human creativity and the artists behind original work.

Instead, HauteBot focuses on ideas, references, and conversation. All powered by publicly available data, including fashion pieces from the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s open-access repository
We built this tool because we believe design shouldn’t feel like a dead-end when inspiration runs low. It should feel like a dialogue — and now, it can.
